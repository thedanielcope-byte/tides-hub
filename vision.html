Vision, Mission, Core Values

The principles that guide every decision we make at Tides Bookkeeping.

Vision

What is the ultimate future we want to create in 3–10 years?

Be the go-to bookkeeping partner for service-based businesses, known for seamless onboarding, transparent pricing, and proactive financial guidance.

  • Serve 200+ clients across real estate, legal, travel, healthcare, and consulting industries with a fully automated, scalable operations model.
  • Build a business that runs without the owner — systematized, delegated, and profitable.
Mission

Why does our company exist? Who do we serve, and how do we make their lives better today?

  • We exist to give service-based business owners clarity and confidence in their finances — so they can focus on running their business, not chasing receipts.
  • We serve entrepreneurs in real estate, legal, travel, email, healthcare, consulting, marketing, home services, and technology with clear communication.
  • We make their lives better by handling the bookkeeping they don't have time for — accurately, on time, and with a friendly team they can actually reach.
Core Values

What principles guide our team's behavior and decisions every day?

⚡ Accuracy over speed
Get it right the first time. Errors in bookkeeping cost clients time and money — precision is non-negotiable.
📣 Proactive communication
No surprises. Flag issues early, update clients before they have to ask, and keep everyone in the loop.
✨ Simplicity
Make finances easy to understand. If a client can't follow it, we haven't done our job well enough.
🤝 Ownership
Treat every client's books like our own business. Take full responsibility — no blame-shifting, no excuses.
⚙️ Systematize everything
If we do it twice, automate it. Build systems that scale so our team spends time on high-value work, not repetitive tasks.
