Brand Guidelines — Do's & Don'ts

✅ Do's

  • Use primary brand colors consistently across all materials
  • Speak in plain language — avoid jargon
  • Lead with client benefits, not features
  • Use the logo with sufficient clear space
  • Keep messaging warm and encouraging
  • Always proofread financial figures before publishing

❌ Don'ts

  • Don't stretch, distort, or recolor the logo
  • Don't use fonts outside the brand typography set
  • Don't use overly technical accounting language with clients
  • Don't place the logo on busy or low-contrast backgrounds
  • Don't use negative or fear-based messaging
  • Don't publish content without brand review
